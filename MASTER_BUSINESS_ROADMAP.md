## 📽️ Phase 3: Project Workflows (The Revenue Engine)

### 📞 The Call Project Workflow
1.  **Search & Vetting**: Filter Airtable → Send vetting questions via Instantly.ai.
2.  **The Blurb**: Send a professional summary of the expert's relevance to the client.
3.  **Compliance**: **Protocol 7 T&Cs** must be signed via DocuSign/HelloSign before the call link is shared.
4.  **Attestation**: sign 1-click "Post-Call Form" confirming no MNPI was shared.

### 📋 The Institutional Survey Workflow
1.  **The 3-Redirect Architecture**:
    *   `/compliance/success` (Complete -> Status: Paid)
    *   `/compliance/screenout` (Terminate -> Status: Unpaid)
    *   `/compliance/status` (Overquota -> Status: Unpaid)
2.  **Unique Linking**: Append `&UID=SBI_XXX` to the survey URL so Qualtrics tracks exactly who to pay.

## 💸 Phase 4: Financial Execution & Cash Flow
